    "\n",
    "At the time of writing of this tutorial (Scpy v.0.2), spectrochempy has the following readers which are specific\n",

    "\n",

    "- `read_matlab()` to open .mat files\n",
    "\n",
    "## Import of OMNIC files\n",
    "\n",
