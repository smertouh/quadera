    "## Internal links\n",
    "To link to a section within your notebook, use the following code:\n",
    "```\n",
    "[Section title](#section-title)\n",
    "```\n",
    "\n",
    "For the text inside the parentheses, replace any spaces and special characters with a hyphen. For example,\n",
    "if your section is called `processing_functions`, you'd enter:\n",
    "```\n",
    "[processing_functions](#processing_functions)\n",
    "```\n",
    "[processing_functions](#processing_functions)\n",
    "\n",
    "Alternatively, you can add an ID above the section:\n",
    "```\n",
    "<a id=\"section_ID\"></a>\n",
    "```\n",
    "\n",
    "**Important**\n",
    "Each ID in the notebook must be unique.\n",
    "\n",
    "To link to a section that has an ID, use the following code:\n",
    "```\n",
    "[Section title](#section_ID)\n",
    "```\n",
    "[Section title](#section_ID)"
